# Appointment Scheduling Guide

## Overview
SafeSpace makes it easy to schedule, manage, and join therapy appointments. This guide covers everything you need to know about the appointment system.

## Getting Started

### Who Can Schedule Appointments?
- **Guardians:** Can schedule appointments for their children
- **Therapists:** Can set availability and manage their schedule
- **Admins:** Can schedule any type of appointment
- **Children:** Can view their appointments and join meetings

## Scheduling an Appointment

### For Guardians

#### Step 1: Access the Scheduling System
1. Log into your SafeSpace account
2. Navigate to "Appointments" in the main menu
3. Click "Schedule New Appointment"

#### Step 2: Select Appointment Type
- Choose the type of session you need
- Consider who should participate
- Select appropriate duration

#### Step 3: Choose Your Therapist
- View available therapists
- See their specializations and availability
- Read their profiles and reviews

#### Step 4: Select Date and Time
- View the therapist's available time slots
- Choose a date that works for your schedule
- Consider your child's school schedule and energy levels

#### Step 5: Add Participants
- **Individual Session:** Automatically includes therapist and your child
- **Family Session:** Add family members who should participate
- **Consultation:** Just you and the therapist

#### Step 6: Provide Details
- Add a brief description of what you'd like to focus on
- Include any special requests or concerns
- Note any scheduling preferences

#### Step 7: Submit Request
- Review all details
- Submit the appointment request
- Wait for therapist confirmation

### For Therapists

#### Managing Your Availability
1. Go to "Therapist Dashboard"
2. Click "Manage Availability"
3. Set your regular weekly schedule
4. Add specific available time slots
5. Block out unavailable times (holidays, breaks)

#### Responding to Appointment Requests
1. Review incoming appointment requests
2. Check the details and participant list
3. Approve or suggest alternative times
4. Add any preparation notes

## Appointment Confirmation Process

### Automatic Confirmations
Some appointments are automatically confirmed:
- Recurring appointments with established clients
- Appointments within your regular availability
- Emergency or urgent sessions

### Manual Confirmations
Most appointments require therapist approval:
- First-time appointments with new clients
- Appointments outside regular hours
- Group or family sessions requiring coordination

### Confirmation Timeline
- **Standard Requests:** Confirmed within 24 hours
- **Urgent Requests:** Confirmed within 4 hours
- **Emergency Requests:** Confirmed within 1 hour

## Managing Your Appointments

### Viewing Your Schedule
**Calendar View:**
- See all appointments in a monthly calendar
- Color-coded by appointment type
- Click any appointment for details

**List View:**
- See upcoming appointments in chronological order
- Filter by date range or appointment type
- Search for specific appointments

### Appointment Details
Each appointment shows:
- Date, time, and duration
- Participants and their roles
- Meeting link (when available)
- Appointment notes and goals
- Preparation materials

### Modifying Appointments

#### Rescheduling
1. Open the appointment details
2. Click "Reschedule"
3. Select a new date/time from available slots
4. Add a reason for the change
5. Submit the request

**Rescheduling Rules:**
- Must be done at least 24 hours in advance
- Subject to therapist availability
- May require re-confirmation

#### Cancelling
1. Open the appointment details
2. Click "Cancel Appointment"
3. Select a cancellation reason
4. Confirm the cancellation

**Cancellation Policy:**
- 24+ hours notice: No penalty
- Less than 24 hours: May count as a missed session
- Emergency cancellations: Contact support

## Joining Your Appointment

### Before the Session

#### 30 Minutes Before:
- Check your email for the meeting link
- Test your camera and microphone
- Find a quiet, private space
- Gather any materials mentioned by your therapist

#### 15 Minutes Before:
- Close unnecessary applications
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Have water and tissues nearby
- Silence your phone

### Joining the Meeting

#### From SafeSpace Platform:
1. Go to "My Appointments"
2. Find your current appointment
3. Click "Join Meeting"
4. Allow camera and microphone access

#### From Email Link:
1. Open the appointment reminder email
2. Click the "Join Meeting" link
3. This will open Google Meet
4. Allow necessary permissions

#### Technical Requirements:
- **Internet:** Stable broadband connection
- **Browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- **Devices:** Computer, tablet, or smartphone
- **Permissions:** Camera and microphone access

### During the Session

#### Best Practices:
- **Be Present:** Focus on the session, avoid distractions
- **Participate Actively:** Engage in discussions and activities
- **Respect Privacy:** Don't record or screenshot
- **Stay Connected:** Keep your camera on unless requested otherwise

#### Technical Issues:
- **Audio Problems:** Check microphone settings, try refreshing
- **Video Problems:** Check camera permissions, restart browser
- **Connection Issues:** Try rejoining the meeting
- **Persistent Issues:** Contact support immediately

## Appointment Reminders

### Email Reminders
You'll receive automatic email reminders:
- **24 hours before:** Appointment confirmation with details
- **1 hour before:** Final reminder with meeting link
- **15 minutes before:** Last-minute reminder (optional)

### In-App Notifications
- Dashboard notifications for upcoming appointments
- Push notifications on mobile devices
- Calendar integration reminders

### Customizing Reminders
1. Go to "Settings" > "Email Preferences"
2. Adjust appointment reminder settings
3. Choose reminder timing preferences
4. Enable/disable specific reminder types

## Special Appointment Types

### Emergency Sessions
**When to Request:**
- Crisis situations requiring immediate support
- Urgent mental health concerns
- Safety-related issues

**How to Request:**
1. Use the "Emergency Appointment" button
2. Describe the urgent situation
3. Provide contact information
4. Wait for immediate response (usually within 1 hour)

### Recurring Appointments
**Setting Up:**
1. Schedule your first appointment normally
2. During confirmation, select "Make Recurring"
3. Choose frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)
4. Set end date or number of sessions

**Managing Recurring Appointments:**
- Modify individual sessions without affecting the series
- Cancel the entire series if needed
- Adjust frequency as treatment progresses

### Group Sessions
**Joining Group Sessions:**
- You'll be invited to appropriate groups
- Groups are organized by age, concern, or treatment type
- Respect group confidentiality rules

**Group Session Etiquette:**
- Arrive on time
- Respect others' sharing time
- Maintain confidentiality
- Follow group guidelines

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "No Available Time Slots"
**Solutions:**
- Try a different therapist
- Look at dates further in the future
- Contact the therapist directly
- Consider different appointment types

#### "Meeting Link Not Working"
**Solutions:**
- Check your email for the correct link
- Try refreshing the page
- Use a different browser
- Contact support for a new link

#### "Appointment Not Confirmed"
**Possible Reasons:**
- Therapist hasn't reviewed the request yet
- Scheduling conflict needs resolution
- Additional information required
- Technical issue with the system

### Getting Help

#### In-App Support:
- Click "Help" on any appointment page
- Use the live chat feature
- Submit a support ticket

#### Phone Support:
- Call during business hours for urgent issues
- Have your appointment details ready
- Explain the specific problem clearly

#### Email Support:
- Send details to support@safespace.app
- Include appointment ID and error messages
- Attach screenshots if helpful

## Privacy and Security

### Meeting Security
- All meetings use encrypted connections
- Meeting links are unique and expire after use
- Only invited participants can join
- Meetings are not recorded by default

### Data Protection
- Appointment details are stored securely
- Only authorized users can access your information
- You can request data deletion at any time
- All staff follow strict confidentiality protocols

### Confidentiality
- What's shared in sessions stays confidential
- Therapists follow professional ethical guidelines
- Group session participants agree to confidentiality
- Emergency situations may require breaking confidentiality for safety
