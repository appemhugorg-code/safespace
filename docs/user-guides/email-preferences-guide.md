# Email Preferences Management Guide

## Overview
SafeSpace allows you to customize which email notifications you receive to ensure you stay informed about what matters most to you while avoiding unnecessary emails.

## Accessing Email Preferences

### Web Interface
1. Log into your SafeSpace account
2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner
3. Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu
4. Click on "Email Preferences" in the sidebar

### Direct Link
You can also access your email preferences directly at: `/settings/email-preferences`

## Managing Your Preferences

### Enabling Notifications
1. Navigate to Email Preferences
2. Toggle the switch **ON** (blue) for the notification types you want to receive
3. Click "Save Preferences" at the bottom of the page

### Disabling Notifications
1. Navigate to Email Preferences
2. Toggle the switch **OFF** (gray) for the notification types you don't want
3. Click "Save Preferences" at the bottom of the page

### Bulk Management
- **Enable All:** Click "Enable All Notifications" to receive all available emails
- **Disable All:** Click "Disable All" (Emergency Alerts will remain enabled)

## Email Frequency Settings

### Immediate Notifications
Most notifications are sent immediately when events occur (appointments, messages, emergencies).

### Digest Mode (Coming Soon)
- **Daily Digest:** Receive a summary of the day's activities
- **Weekly Digest:** Receive a weekly summary of important updates

## Unsubscribing from Emails

### From Email Links
Every non-emergency email includes an "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom. Clicking this will:
1. Take you to a preferences page
2. Allow you to unsubscribe from that specific email type
3. Give you the option to manage all preferences

### Complete Unsubscribe
To stop all non-emergency emails:
1. Go to Email Preferences
2. Click "Unsubscribe from All"
3. Confirm your choice

**Important:** You will still receive emergency alerts and critical security notifications.

## Troubleshooting

### Not Receiving Expected Emails?

**Check Your Preferences:**
1. Verify the notification type is enabled in your preferences
2. Ensure your email address is correct in your profile

**Check Your Email Provider:**
1. Look in your spam/junk folder
2. Add `noreply@safespace.app` to your contacts
3. Check if your email provider has blocked SafeSpace emails

**Still Having Issues?**
Contact support with:
- Your account email address
- Which notifications you're missing
- When you last received emails from SafeSpace

### Receiving Too Many Emails?

**Adjust Your Preferences:**
1. Disable non-essential notification types
2. Consider switching to digest mode (when available)
3. Unsubscribe from marketing emails if not interested

### Email Delivery Delays?

**Normal Delays:**
- Appointment reminders: Sent exactly 24h and 1h before appointments
- Content notifications: Sent when new content is published
- Message notifications: Sent within 5 minutes of receiving a message

**Unusual Delays:**
If emails are consistently delayed by more than 30 minutes, please contact support.

## Privacy and Security

### Email Content
- SafeSpace emails never include sensitive personal information
- Meeting links are only included in appointment reminders
- All emails are sent through encrypted connections

### Data Protection
- Your email preferences are stored securely
- We never share your email address with third parties
- You can request deletion of your email data at any time

### Unsubscribe Rights
- You can unsubscribe from any non-emergency emails at any time
- Unsubscribing is immediate and permanent until you re-enable notifications
- Emergency alerts cannot be disabled for safety reasons
